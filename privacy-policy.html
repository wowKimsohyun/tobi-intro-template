Privacy Policy: Elements of AI

This privacy policy explains what personal data Reaktor and University of Helsinki collect on you when you sign up for and participate in the Elements of AI courses and how such data is used.

Should you have any questions or requests regarding the processing of your personal data in relation to the course by either of us, please send such questions to [email protected] or [email protected]

1. Data controllers

The data controllers regarding the data you provide upon signing up for and participating in the course are:

Reaktor Innovations Oy (“Reaktor”)

Yliopistonkatu 4, 00100 Helsinki, Finland

tel. +358 9 415 202 00

And

University of Helsinki (“University of Helsinki”)

Fabianinkatu 33 (P.O. Box 33),

FI-00014 University of Helsinki, Finland

Reaktor and University of Helsinki herein also “we” and “us”.

2. Categories of personal data and purposes of processing the personal data

When you sign up for and participate in the course we may ask you to provide us with the following information:

  • Your name
  • Password
  • Email
  • Country of origin
  • Zip code
  • Student number (if applicable)

We use this information in order to send notifications regarding the course (e.g. status messages), process applications to the course and manage your participation in the course.

During the course we will collect data on tasks and exercises in the course. All data is processed to allow us to provide the course, follow your progress and confirm the completion of the course. We also compile statistics on participation in the course.

We may also process your data for research purposes and for the purpose of sending information on new courses if you have given your consent for such processing.

After completing the course, we keep your user account active in order to make information on the course progress and the course certificate available to you. However, you may at any time request us to remove your user account and delete your data.

3. Provision of data to third parties

Your data is processed by the personnel at Reaktor, its group companies and University of Helsinki who are required to access such data to organise the course and perform the tasks described herein.

If you wish to have credits from your own university who is organising the course with us, we can also share your data with such university to the extent this is necessary for such credits.

4. Requests regarding the data

You have the right to request accessing, erasing and correcting your personal data processed by us as well as send us any other requests based on the applicable laws such as request restriction of or object the processing of your personal data. Further, you may prohibit us from using your data at any time. Please send any requests concerning your data to [email protected] and/or [email protected].

5. Transfer of data outside of the EU and EEA

We use Zapier to integrate our emails from the course servers to our email management system which is MailChimp, a U.S. based email marketing platform. Therefore, personal data will be transferred outside of the EU and EEA to the U.S. as required for the application processing. We have taken measures to ensure that such transfer of data complies with the applicable laws by applying appropriate security measures and signing standard contractual clauses based on the EU Commission’s applicable decision with our service provider. For more information on processing of personal data by Zapier and Mailchimp, please see: Zapier Privacy Shield Information, MailChimp Privacy Shield Information.

We also use the following third party service providers to provide analytics for the course and our website:

Google Tag Manager — We use Google Tag Manager to manage our analytics platforms.

Google Analytics — We use Google Analytics to collect information about how users behave on our courses and platform. We use the information to compile reports and to help us improve the site. These cookies collect the number of visitors to the website, where visitors have come to the website from and the pages they visited. You can read more about these cookies and how Google safeguards them here.

Hotjar — We use Hotjar to validate that our website design and user experience are performing as intended. Hotjar Privacy Policy

Google Data Studio — we use Google Data Studio to help us improve the site by structuring it in more accessible manner and we might share these reports and data included within them with our partner universities.

The above described service providers are located outside of the EU and EEA. In these cases, we have ensured that the transfer of data complies with the applicable laws by applying appropriate security measures and signing standard contractual clauses based on EU Commission’s applicable decision with the respective service provider.

7. Other services related to the course

In addition, you can use Reddit and Spectrum as the providers of our online course chat communities which enable self-guided reinforcement of learnings from the course by sharing learnings and experiences related to the course in a community of student peers. The chat communities can be used for setting up study groups and community chats for the purpose of discussing questions related to the course, learnings, and applications for topics related to the course. These services are optional. We do not share any data to Reddit or Spectrum. For more information on collection and processing of personal data in Reddit and Spectrum, see Reddit Privacy Policy; Spectrum Privacy Policy

8. Data security

All data collected by Reaktor and University of Helsinki is processed confidentially. Access to the data is made available only to a selected group of our employees and is only processed for the purposes described herein. The network and equipment used in processing have been secured using appropriate measures.

9. Other

Data protection laws are supervised in Finland by the Finnish Data Protection Ombudsman. If you wish to lodge a complaint regarding processing of personal data or if you have other questions belonging to the jurisdiction of the Data Protection Ombudsman, you may contact the Data Protection Ombudsman’s office as advised in www.tietosuoja.fi.

10. Account deletion

If you want to delete your account, please do the following steps:

1. Log into your account at http://tmc.mooc.fi/

2. Go to settings in your account view and request an account removal there

Please note that removal of the user account causes erasure of information related to the course progress and, for example, course certification.
